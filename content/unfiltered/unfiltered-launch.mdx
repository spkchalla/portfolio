Welcome to **Unfiltered**.

This is a space where I'll be sharing things that don't necessarily fit into technical research or project logs. Here, I'll talk about:

- **Interests**: Movies, books, music, and hobbies.
- **Thoughts & Ideas**: Random shower thoughts or deep dives into specific topics.
- **Opinions**: My take on various subjects, from technology trends to life philosophy.
- **Feelings**: Reflections on personal growth and experiences.

I wanted a place to be more personal and less structured. I hope you find something here that resonates with you.
